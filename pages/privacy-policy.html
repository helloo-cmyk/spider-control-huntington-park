PRIVACY POLICY

Last updated: January 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Huntington Park Spider Control ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and shares your personal information when you use our website.

1. Information We Collect

When you contact us for a quote or service, we may collect personal information such as your name, phone number, email address, and physical address. We also collect basic usage data through cookies to improve our website experience.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to connect you with local pest control professionals, communicate with you regarding your service requests, and improve our services. Your contact information is essential for dispatching technicians to your property.

3. Sharing of Information

We share your information with our network of certified local pest control technicians to fulfill your service requests. We do not sell your personal data to third parties for unrelated marketing purposes.

4. Your Rights

Depending on your location, such as California, you may have specific rights regarding your personal data under laws like the CCPA. You have the right to request access to, deletion of, or opt-out of the sale of your personal information.
