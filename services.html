How Our Treatment Works

We target spiders where they breed and hide, avoiding aggressive applications that expose your family to chemicals.

1

Eaves & Siding Sweeping

We physically brush down all siding, trim, windowsills, and eaves to clear active webs and egg sacs.

2

Crack & Crevice Dusting

We apply dry, insecticidal dust deep inside structural cracks, wall voids, and brick crevices where spiders hide.

3

Perimeter Defense Barriers

We spray a safe, water-resistant residual treatment around the foundation, doorways, and baseboards to form a persistent barrier.

Eco-Friendly & Family-Safe Solutions

Your health and safety are our main concern. We prioritize eco-conscious products derived from natural pyrethrins and botanical oils. These formulas target the nervous systems of spiders and insects but are completely safe for children, dogs, cats, and other domestic pets once the application has dried. We follow strict EPA regulations and apply products only in targeted cracks, voids, and perimeter locations, minimizing exposure to high-traffic areas of your property.
