## Slide 2: Who Are We Talking To? (Demographics)
**Slide Headline:** Understanding the Huntington Park Resident
**On-Screen Text:**
* Dense Population: ~55,000 residents in 3 sq. miles
* Highly Diverse: 97% Hispanic/Latino demographic
* Family-Oriented: High concentration of families with children
* Working Class: Values trust and fair pricing over luxury

**Speaker Notes:**
> "First, we have to understand our audience. Huntington Park is a dense, vibrant, working-class city. It's 97% Hispanic with a high number of young families. Because this is a hardworking community, our marketing cannot sound like a giant, expensive corporation. Our messaging must focus heavily on transparency, fair pricing, and trust to win their business."

## Slide 3: The Environmental Factors
**Slide Headline:** Why Huntington Park Needs Us (The Weather Factor)
**On-Screen Text:**
* Climate: Mild, Mediterranean
* Daily Pattern: Coastal morning fog clearing to warm afternoons
* Result: Ideal moisture conditions for insects and spiders

**Speaker Notes:**
> "Why is spider control such a big deal here? It comes down to the weather. Huntington Park has a very specific climate pattern—we get that coastal morning fog that burns off by the afternoon. This daily cycle of moisture creates the perfect micro-climate for insects. And where there are insects, predator spiders like Wolf Spiders and Black Widows thrive, especially in the residential gardens."

## Slide 4: Hyper-Local Targeting (GEO Signals)
**Slide Headline:** Leveraging Local Landmarks
**On-Screen Text:**
* **Pacific Boulevard:** The commercial heart of the city
* **Salt Lake Park:** High vegetation = higher pest pressure
* **The Bungalow District:** Older wood-frame homes

**Speaker Notes:**
> "To beat national competitors on Google, we have to prove we are local. We do this by dropping hyper-local 'GEO signals' into our website copy. For our commercial services, we talk about keeping Pacific Boulevard shops clean and pest-free. For residential, we talk about how the older, wood-frame homes in the Bungalow District are prime real estate for Black Widows. This proves to both Google and the customer that we actually know the area."

**Slide Headline:** Speaking Their Language
